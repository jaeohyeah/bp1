Installation Tips for Beginners

So, you’ve got your brand-new bypass filter kit. Awesome! Now let’s get it set up. If you’ve ever built something with blocks or LEGO, this will feel pretty familiar. We’re just putting some pieces together to help your engine stay clean and happy.

What You’ll Need

  • Your bypass filter kit
  • A wrench set (like the tool your parents use to tighten bolts)
  • A clean cloth or rag
  • New engine oil (just in case you spill a little)
  • An adult (always a good idea!)

Step 1: Pick a Safe Spot

Open your car’s hood and find a nice, flat place to mount the bypass filter. Usually, it’s near the engine or on the side where there’s enough space.

Step 2: Mount the Bracket

Use the included bracket to hold the filter. It’s like clipping a lunchbox to the side of your backpack—it keeps the filter stable while the car moves.

Step 3: Connect the Hoses

The kit comes with hoses that carry oil in and out of the filter. Attach them like you would snap together two straws. Make sure they're tight, so nothing leaks!

Step 4: Check for Leaks

Start the engine and look closely. If you see any drips, turn off the engine and tighten the hose a bit more. Just like making sure your water bottle lid is on tight!

Step 5: Give Yourself a High-Five

You just helped your engine get a superhero filter that’ll keep the oil clean. You did a great job!
