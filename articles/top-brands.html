Top Bypass Filter Brands in 2025

There are tons of bypass filters out there, but which ones really shine in 2025? We've rounded up a few popular picks that mechanics and truck owners are loving right now. Let's see what makes each one special.

1. AMSOIL

AMSOIL's bypass filter systems are trusted for their long drain intervals and ultra-fine filtration. Their filters can remove particles as small as 2 microns!

  • High-efficiency cellulose media
  • Durable, corrosion-resistant construction
  • Backed by strong user reviews

2. Frantz Filters

Frantz Filters have been around for decades and are known for their toilet-paper-style filter media — simple but effective. They're budget-friendly and easy to maintain.

  • Classic design
  • Great for DIY installers
  • Low-cost replacement filters

3. Fleetguard (Cummins Filtration)

Made for heavy-duty engines, Fleetguard filters are a top choice for trucks and commercial vehicles. They're designed to handle high flow rates with precision.

  • Trusted by fleet operators
  • OEM-grade engineering
  • Excellent for diesel engines

4. Baldwin Filters

Baldwin’s bypass filters are built to last, with rugged canisters and fine filtration. They're used in agriculture, mining, and industrial settings.

  • Extra-durable housing
  • Resistant to extreme conditions
  • Long replacement intervals

Every engine is a little different, so the best bypass filter brand for you depends on how (and where) you drive. But with any of these options, you’re off to a great start!
